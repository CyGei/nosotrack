NosoTrack

A Real-Time Platform for Nosocomial Outbreak Analytics

Healthcare-associated infections (HAIs) affect 2 million Americans annually, causing 100,000 deaths and 8 million additional hospital days, costing over $40 billion. Preventable outbreaks driven by person to person transmitted pathogens such as SARS-CoV-2, Clostridioides difficile, Klebsiella pneumoniae, and MRSA generate the most acute clinical and operational crises, triggering staff shortages, ward closures, and delayed procedures. Current response practice relies on broad testing and isolation protocols. However, effective control depends on identifying the specific drivers of an outbreak: 'patient 0', modes and locations of spread, superspreading events, and undetected or imported cases.
NosoTrack is an outbreak analytics platform that infers who infected whom in near real-time by integrating data from hospital Electronic Health Records with Bluetooth enabled contact tracing.
